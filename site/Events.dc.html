Welcome to Box House Events
Brooklyn's most unique rooftop venue
Perched atop Greenpoint's Box House Hotel, our charming rooftop venue offers exceptional service complemented by stunning views of New York City. Elevate your event with our modern ambiance, world-class hospitality and comprehensive in-house offerings: expert design and planning, seamless day-of coordination, and a Michelin-recognized culinary team. Whether you're hosting a corporate gathering or a dream wedding, let us help make your event truly unforgettable.
