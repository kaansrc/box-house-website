# WELCOME

---

The Box House Hotel offers an authentic Brooklyn experience. Located just off the East River in Brooklyn’s northernmost Greenpoint neighborhood, the Box House Hotel is a boutique hotel that gives you the feel of home, just minutes from the excitement of Midtown Manhattan.

# THE TEAM

---

The team stands by its core principals of *quality, creativity, service and hospitality*. Our goal is to always provide our guests with an unforgettable experience that adds value to your trip in a stylish environment.

# OUR LOCATION

---

Situated in Greenpoint, The Box House Hotel is located in a unique Brooklyn community bordering North Williamsburg and Long Island City that offers numerous private and public transportation options including the NYC subway, Citibike sharing, and the delightful East River Ferry. Greenpoint’s streets are teeming with restaurants, bars, cafés, bakeries, and stylish new boutiques. The neighborhood is also home to numerous independent art galleries, music venues, parks, and playgrounds. Our slice of the Brooklyn waterfront is only a short walk from the hotel, and features stellar views of Manhattan.
