# Accessibility

We provide an exceptional experience and ensure accessibility for all guests.

Accessibility features include:

-Accessible entrance with ramp

-Accessible route to guest rooms and on-site restaurant

-Accessible King and Queen guest rooms

-32″ door width

-Roll in shower with grab bars

-Lowered light switches for wheelchair access

-Lowered toilet seats with grab bars

If you wish to discuss Accessibility features with us, please telephone our reservations desk at the following number: 718-383-3800
