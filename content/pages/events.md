### **TOP OF THE BOX**

Our fully retractable rooftop features 10,000 sq ft of unobstructed panoramic Manhattan, Brooklyn and Long Island City views. The Rooftop was designed to service any event and features a wrap-around marble bar, heating, air-conditioning, and is completely encapsulated with glass sliding doors. All this makes it an event space for every day of the year, rain or shine.

We are currently closed to the public. Please click **[here](https://resy.com/cities/ny/top-of-the-box?date=2020-08-01&seats=2)** to make a reservation on the few select dates we will be open. To preview the menu, please click **[here](https://topofthebox.menu/10/).**

10,000 sq ft • 293 person capacity • 4 restrooms • heating • air-conditioning • glass sliding door enclosure • live green wall • 2 elevators • string lighting • audio and lighting • handicap accessible

### **PENTHOUSE**

Located directly beneath the rooftop, the [Penthouse](https://theboxhousehotel.com/room/penthouse-suite/) is 2,500 sq ft of preparation or pre-party photography space. It’s the perfect space for intimate dinner parties or cocktail parties as well.

2 master bedrooms • 2.5 bathrooms • lounge furniture • full kitchen/bar • city view • 3 wrap-around balconies • tin ceilings • 3 flat Screen TVs • surround sound speakers

### **BROOKLYN LANTERN**

[Brooklyn Lantern](https://www.bklynlantern.com/) is a full-service restaurant & bar specializing in creative American cuisine, craft and local beer, and imaginative cocktails. Our chefs believe in organic and sustainable farming and utilize only the freshest ingredients to create savory meals and drinks served a refined and vibrant environment. The perfect destination for an after-party, cocktail party, movie screening or post-wedding brunch.

2,000 sq ft • 50 seated • 125 standing • Moroccan tiles • projector • surround sound speakers • iPod station
