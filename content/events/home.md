## About Us

Welcome to Box House Events. Perched atop Greenpoint's Box House Hotel, our charming rooftop venue offers exceptional service complemented by stunning views of New York City. Elevate your event with our modern ambiance, world-class hospitality and comprehensive in-house offerings—including expert design and planning, seamless day-of coordination, and a Michelin-recognized culinary team. Whether you're hosting a corporate gathering or a dream wedding, let us help make your event truly unforgettable. Start planning with us today.

**Location:** The Box House Hotel · 77 Box Street · Brooklyn, NY 11222

**All-inclusive venue suited for up to 300 guests.**

## Event Types

- **Weddings** — Expert in-house planning and Michelin-recognized catering teams
- **Corporate Events** — Breathtaking views of NYC from our fully retractable rooftop. The perfect setting for any occasion
- **Private Parties**

## The Venue — The Box House Hotel Rooftop

Our rooftop venue sets the stage for unforgettable events. From corporate soirées to dreamy weddings, elevate your experience with our stylish ambiance and impeccable service. Let the skyline be your witness.

## Brooklyn's Most Unique Venue

Our fully retractable rooftop venue is perched atop The Box House Hotel at 77 Box Street in Greenpoint, Brooklyn. Our space offers breathtaking skyline views and seamless climate control, creating the perfect backdrop for any occasion.
