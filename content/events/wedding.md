# Weddings

## Backed by Brooklyn's Room 11 Hospitality

Step into our all-inclusive rooftop venue, where our dedicated team will expertly plan and execute your dream wedding.

Your event is planned and run by Room 11 Hospitality, our Brooklyn family of hospitality brands. We handle it end to end: MICHELIN-recognized catering from **MADRE**, full planning and day-of coordination from the Box House Events team, room blocks at our three partner hotels for your guests, and in-house sound, lighting, and video production from **Room 11 Productions**. One family, one standard, from your first tour to the last song of the night.

## Venue Specs

- 10,000 sq. ft of space
- 400 person capacity*
- Retractable rooftop
- Seamless climate control
- Marble wrap-around bar
- Four restrooms
- ADA accessible
- Three elevators
- Sliding glass windows

## What we offer as an all-inclusive venue

- Access to our team of expert planners & designers
- In-house catering and servers
- Premium open bar options
- Tables and chairs
- Flatware & glassware
- Votive candles
- Room blocks
